Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our product

Is the electronic lice machine suitable for all age groups?

Yes, our electronic lice machine is designed for all age groups 4 years and older. For young children, adult supervision is recommended.

Is there any pain when using the electronic lice machine?

No. During usage, when lices are exposed to the right amount of heated air at the right temperature and for the right length of time, they dry out and die.

How often should I use it for best results?

When lice are detected, we recommend to use as soon as possible, then 1-2 times yearly for prevention. In high-risk environments like schools, usage frequency can be increased.

Can the electronic lice machine completely replace chemical treatments?

In most cases, our electronic lice machine can completely replace chemical treatments. For particularly severe lice infestations, it may need to be used in conjunction with other methods.

Does the product have a warranty?

Yes, depending on the package you purchase, we offer 12-24 months warranty. During the warranty period, free repair or replacement is available for quality issues.

How do I clean the electronic lice machine?

After each use, please use the included cleaning brush to remove residue between the teeth. You can gently wipe the teeth with an alcohol swab, but do not immerse the entire device in water or other liquids. This will ensure your lice machine remains in optimal condition.
