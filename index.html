About Lice Machine

Lice Machine is an exhilarating monster survivor game that puts your skills and strategy to the test. As waves of creepy creatures attack from all directions, you must navigate the battlefield, collect power-ups, and survive as long as possible. With each passing minute, the challenge intensifies, requiring quick reflexes and tactical thinking.

Game Features

  • Challenging monster waves that increase in difficulty
  • Multiple unique characters with different abilities
  • Power-ups and special weapons to discover
  • Endless gameplay with progressive difficulty
  • Compete for high scores and survival time

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose upgrades when leveling up
  • Survive as long as possible against endless waves

Game Strategy Tips

Mastering Lice Machine requires both skill and strategy. Here are some tips to help you survive longer:

Prioritize Movement

Always keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Circular patterns often work best to manage large groups of enemies.

Strategic Upgrades

Focus on a few powerful abilities rather than spreading upgrades across many different weapons.

Resource Management

Collect health pickups when you need them most, and try to pick up experience orbs efficiently.
