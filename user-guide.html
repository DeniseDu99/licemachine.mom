How to Use the Electronic Lice Machine

This video will guide you through the complete process of correctly using our electronic lice machine, from powering on to cleaning. By following the steps demonstrated in the video, you will be able to effectively remove lice and nits from hair.

Detailed Instructions

1Preparation

Before using the device, ensure that it is fully charged. Check that the comb attachment is clean and properly attached to the device. It's best to use the device on dry hair to maximize effectiveness.

2Powering On

Press the power button located on the handle of the device. The indicator light will turn on, showing that the device is ready to use. The device will emit a gentle humming sound when operating correctly.

3Treatment Process

Start from the roots of the hair, slowly moving the device through the hair strands. Make sure the comb teeth touch the scalp gently. Divide the hair into sections and methodically work through each section to ensure complete coverage.

Continue this process until you have treated the entire scalp. Pay special attention to areas behind the ears and at the nape of the neck, as these are common locations for lice infestations.

Pro Tip

For best results, use the device every 3-4 days for two weeks to ensure all newly hatched lice are eliminated. Regular use will help prevent reinfestation.

4Cleaning After Use

After each use, turn off the device and carefully remove the comb attachment. Clean the comb thoroughly with the cleaning brush provided, removing any trapped lice or debris. You can also rinse the comb attachment with warm water, but ensure it is completely dry before reattaching it to the device.

5Storage

Store the device in a clean, dry place. Keep it away from water and extreme temperatures.

Important Note

Do not share the device among different users without thoroughly cleaning it first to prevent cross-contamination.
